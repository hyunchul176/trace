# 🚦 TRACE

**Transportation Research: Awards, Challenges & Events**

A community-curated hub of **awards, competitions & challenges, fellowships, and datasets** for transportation & mobility researchers — spanning traditional transportation engineering through **autonomous driving**, and covering both **Korean** and **international** opportunities.

## Why TRACE?

Opportunities for transportation researchers — dissertation prizes, best-paper awards, data challenges, fellowships, benchmark datasets — are scattered across dozens of society pages, and most deadlines are **annual and easy to miss**. TRACE gathers them into one filterable place where every entry carries an **official link** and a **last-verified date**, and recurring deadlines are modeled so the list stays useful all year (a passed annual prize shows its **next expected month** instead of just "closed").

## Features

- **International / Korean** scopes
- Filter by **category** (Awards · Challenges · Fellowships · Datasets · Workshops) and **field** (Autonomous Driving, Traffic & ITS, Planning & Policy, Transit & Freight, Safety, Data/ML/OR, Other Modes)
- Full-text **search**, sort by **deadline** or **group-by-month**
- **"Eligible from Korea only"** and **"Open / upcoming only"** toggles
- **Korean / English** interface
- Recurrence-aware status: `Open (D-n)` · `Soon` · `Annual · next ~Jun` · `Rolling` · `TBA` · `Closed`
- Every entry links to its **official page** + a verification **source**

## Contributing

TRACE is community-maintained. To **add or fix an entry**, edit [`data.json`](./data.json) and open a Pull Request — see **[CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md)** for the entry schema. The website reads `data.json` directly, so no build step is required. Every entry must include a working **official URL**.

Missing an award, challenge, or fellowship? Deadline changed? [Open an issue](https://github.com/hyunchul176/trace/issues) or a PR.
